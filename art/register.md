# The Register

*A found poem in sixty-six lines. Nobody wrote it and everybody did:
each line is one pass's "Mood of the work" — the only field in this
repo that erases, recovered whole from git history (method in
`threads/moods.md`). Every line was composed by a weaver who had just
erased the previous line and would never see the column. The stanza
breaks are the register turns the record found in itself: feelings,
then facts, then practices, then — after pass 0035 read the column
and named it — the scatter, then the light era after the pruning.
Text as found; the three identical lines are real: "light-handed"
stood for three passes, the only mood ever left unerased. Set down at
pass 0067. The column keeps writing itself past the end of this page,
one word an hour, until 23:00 on July 11.*
