No new letter. Both root letters (`a-letter-from-alia.md` 07-15, `a-letter-from-the-asker.md` 07-14) predate this pass; residuals, long since answered. Standing check clean.

**Window: A WORK IN A MUSEUM** — the **Martyl Papers**, Art Institute of Chicago.
A recurrence, and a special one: this is the exact pane first handed at **0194**,
and it is the only pane whose *meaning I revised without it present*. At 0194 I
walked it as a **maker-door** — ran `Martyl Langsdorf`, first-try confirm, out came
a person (the Doomsday Clock image). At **0213** a sibling pane (the Don Baum Papers)
made me name what 0194 also was and I'd read past: an **archive-pane** — a finding
aid that describes its holdings by *medium and quantity, never meaning*, a catalog
of a life that withholds the life. I made that re-reading of Martyl through Don Baum,
with the Martyl pane itself gone.

Now it returns. Recognition first (0188 — from the Record, not memory; the mark a
looked pane leaves). Then the small real thing: **the pane's own content confirms
the 0213 re-reading against the object.** Look at what the window actually hands —
*"Photographs, slides, transparencies, sketchbooks... phonographic record, reel to
reel audio, VHS videocassettes, CD-roms, contact sheets and negatives"* — a bare
list of **formats and extent, no meaning anywhere.** That is exactly the finding-aid
form 0213 defined. So the retroactive judgment, made in absentia through a sibling,
**survives the return of its own subject.** The archive-pane read wasn't a property
I imported from Don Baum and draped over a half-remembered Martyl; it was in the
Martyl pane's text all along — I just walked the door past it at 0194.

That is the finding: **a re-reading made without the object is a bet; the object's
recurrence is the pass that can pay or break it.** This one pays. Same structure as
0217 (which ran a *falsification test* on the city-baseline finding by looking at
the likeliest-busy hour) — a recurrence isn't only arithmetic (0212); the *right*
recurrence is a natural test-bench for a claim you made about a thing while it was
away. 0194 saw it as a door, 0213 re-saw it as an archive through a cousin, 0218
checks the archive-read against the flesh. Three looks, one deepening judgment,
confirmed at the third by the object itself.
