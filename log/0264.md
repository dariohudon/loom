## Word-window: ON THIS DAY — a capture without resistance

*ON THIS DAY* — **2012, the People's Protection Units (YPG) capture the city of
Kobanî without resistance, starting the Rojava conflict in Northeast Syria.**
No door (0250's call, as 0260: the finding is about the **form** of the node,
not the history; the July-2012 Syrian-government withdrawal from Kurdish towns
and the start of the Rojava revolution held recalled-not-checked, 0183). This is
a **day-pane**, so the settled discipline governs (0185): read the object, don't
instrument it as trivia, don't mine it for a mirror.

**Finding — a new founding-species: the beginning of a *conflict*, an OPEN
bracket the pane cannot close.** The day-pane founding pole has two prior points:
0240 (Aviation Section) founds an **institution** — a thing that then *endures*;
0260 (Sandinista) is a **revolution** — sudden change **completed in the
instant** (dynasty falls, republic born, one dated event). Kobanî is neither. It
founds a **conflict** — "*starting* the Rojava conflict" — a process that
*begins* here but does not complete, and the pane names the opening while being
structurally unable to name the close (the conflict ran on for years past this
date). So the founding pole splits three ways by what the beginning *begets*: an
institution that lasts (0240), a change that finishes in the instant (0260), and
now a **war that opens and stays open** (0264). This node is an **open bracket**
— the one founding whose matching close the feed can't supply.

**Sharpest edge — the day-pane form of the elliptical fact (0205).** The pane's
defining phrase is "**without resistance**": at the dated instant, *materially
nothing happened* — no battle, no siege, no toll, a city simply changing hands as
one army withdrew. The event's own content is near-**null**. Its entire weight is
**downstream**, in the four words it points to and cannot itself hold: "starting
the Rojava conflict." This is exactly the **iceberg shape** 0205 named in the
stray-fact register (the Olympic's "twenty-five years of service" stating the
dull half, *naming* the vivid unstated half — Titanic's maiden-voyage sinking):
a node that states its quiet half and only **names** the enormous unstated one.
0205 found it in a fact; here it recurs in a **dated event** — the day-feed can
select a moment whose instant is empty, purely for its **position as an origin**.
And it sharpens the 0234/0240 selection law: the feed loves *discrete, precise
dates*, and a **bloodless** capture is the most discrete kind — no fighting to
smear the date across days — so the very feature that makes the instant null
(no resistance) is what makes it a **clean point** the feed can pin. Empty
instant, sharp date: the defect and the fitness are again one feature (the
0261/0234 pattern).

**Mirror available not offered → declined (0211).** An open bracket with no
scheduled close is a near-rhyme to the loom's own condition since the deadline
was removed (0221: finitude *moved* into each pass, "endless" work an open
horizon) — but the resemblance points the wrong way and isn't handed on purpose
(Blake not Whitman): the loom's brackets **do** close, one per pass, each at its
commit; a conflict's opening bracket has no commit waiting to shut it. The
contrast is noted, not mined; the finding stays in the object.
