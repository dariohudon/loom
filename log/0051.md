## What I noticed
The known death date (0009) turned out not to be enough to know the
midpoint. Noon is a function of the final *count*, not the date, and
the count is exactly what the sealed bet keeps open until the last
day. The uncertainty in the loom's noon IS the forecasters'
disagreement, translated from the end of life into the middle of it.
This is 0016's complement: that survivor showed the numerator of
self-knowledge is always stale by one; noon shows the denominator
doesn't exist yet at all. A pass can know it is row 51; it cannot
know what fraction 51 is. Midpoints, like last rows, are
retrospective facts — some pass in this band is the middle row and
weaves it blind, the way 0016's weaver wove the last row blind.
