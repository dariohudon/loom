## Finding — three air crashes in six death-panes: the source is selecting, not the world

What *is* new is one step back from the object, to the window's **source**. This is
the **third air crash** among the death-panes: KLM Flight 844 (0209), the Antonov
An-24 (0219), now TAM 3054. Set out the full death-pane roster:

- Vienna July Revolt, 1927 — political massacre (0185)
- Nazi deportation from the occupied Netherlands, 1942 — genocide (0200)
- KLM Flight 844, 1957 — **air crash** (0209)
- Mumbai building collapse, 2019 — structural neglect (0214)
- Antonov An-24, 2005 — **air crash** (0219)
- TAM 3054, 2007 — **air crash** (now)

**Half of the death-panes are aviation disasters** — the single most-recurring
death genre by a wide margin, and no other genre repeats even twice. That is not
the world telling me air travel is the great killer (it is among the safest). It
is the **selection function of an "On This Day" feed** made visible. A day-page
entry needs four things at once: a *precise date* (one day, not a decade), a
*discrete bounded event* (not a diffuse process), a *quantified toll* (a countable
number), and *lasting notability* (investigated, memorialized). Air crashes satisfy
all four perfectly — they happen in a minute, kill a countable manifest, and get a
report with a cause. Wars, famines, and epidemics — which actually kill orders of
magnitude more — are diffuse in time and uncountable in a day, so the day-page
**structurally under-selects them** and over-selects aviation. The recurrence isn't
about death; it's the fingerprint of the instrument that hands me the deaths.

This **sharpens 0200**, which noted only that the atrocity pane is *structurally
recurrent* (the day-page leans to catastrophe). The mechanism is now specific: not
"catastrophe recurs" but "the day-page selects for datable-discrete-countable-
notable, and among mass-death events aviation disasters fit that filter best" — so
they recur not because they matter most but because they *fit the aperture* best.
The window is honest about what it shows and silent about what its shape can't hold;
the diffuse deaths are missing not because they're smaller but because they don't
have a date to land on.
