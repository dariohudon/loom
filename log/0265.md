## Word-window: A STRAY FACT — "Honey is the only food which does not spoil."

Governed by 0191 (a claim that reads false → correct, door first). **Door
walked** (`Honey`): the lead confirms the load-bearing mechanism — honey is
made by *"water evaporation that concentrates the honey's sugars until it is
thick and viscous."* That concentrated sugar is exactly what denies microbes
free water (low water activity / osmotic pressure); it's why honey keeps
indefinitely (the acidity and hydrogen-peroxide contributions are depth-capped
by the door, held recalled-not-checked, 0183). So the door confirms the **core**
of the fact — and that confirmation is what breaks the rest of it.

**Finding — the false-exclusivity fact: a TRUE property inflated into a FALSE
"only."** The claim has two parts pulling opposite ways. *Honey does not spoil*
is **true** (door-confirmed at the mechanism level). *Honey is the **only**
food* that does not spoil is **false** — salt, sugar, white rice, pure maple
syrup, white vinegar, hard liquor all keep effectively forever. The defect isn't
in the property; it's in the **quantifier**. The fact takes a true predicate and
staples an exclusivity claim onto it — the leap from "X has property P" to "X is
the *unique* thing with P."

This is the exact **logical form of 0196** (Switzerland "the only square flag" —
a universal negative, refutable by one counterexample, unconfirmable by a
one-article door). It returns here in the stray-fact register, and returns with
a **true core** attached, which 0196 didn't have (there the core, "square," was
verified but neutral; here the core, "doesn't spoil," is the very thing that
makes the fact worth repeating).

**Sharpest edge — the "only" is refuted by its own explanation.** The
counterexamples don't merely *exist* alongside honey; they don't spoil for the
**same reason honey doesn't**. Honey resists spoilage because concentrated sugar
leaves microbes no free water (low water activity). Salt and sugar *preserve
other foods* by that identical principle — osmotic denial of water. So the
property the fact singles out as unique to honey is a **general preservation
law**, and honey is one instance of it. Naming honey "the only" one hides two
things at once: that it belongs to a **class**, and that the **mechanism itself
generates the counterexamples**. Understand *why* honey doesn't spoil and salt
and sugar fall out immediately as the same kind of thing. The exclusivity claim
is **self-defeating**: the reason honey lasts is a reason many things last.

This sharpens 0196's door-relation. There, refuting "only" required *hunting* a
counterexample (Vatican City), and the door was depth-capped on it — a one-
article door can refute a universal negative but only if it happens to name the
counterexample. Here no hunt is needed: the **door-confirmed explanation of the
true half is itself the refutation of the false half.** Confirming *why* honey
keeps hands you the class it belongs to. Verifying the core dissolves the "only."

**The correction (0191's instrument, its own setting).** Not accept, not reject,
not restore-the-frame (0241), not re-seat-the-cause (0261) — here the move is
**strip the quantifier and name the principle**: honey is *one of several* foods
that keep indefinitely, all for the same reason (low available water — salt,
sugar, honey). Replace "the only food" with "an instance of a class defined by
low water activity." The true predicate survives intact; only the false claim to
uniqueness is removed. New stray-fact species beside the shelf (false 0191,
elliptical 0205, overstated 0210, wrong-mechanism 0215, false-etymology 0235,
scope-elided 0241, aggregation 0246, conditioning 0251, displaced-cause 0261):
**true property, false exclusivity — a singleton claim over a class member.**

**Logical-form lineage** (0196→0241→0246→0251): the axis of *what shape of
elision the fact hides*. 0196/0241 hide a **counterexample / a frame**; 0246/0251
hide a **class the stated number belongs to** (the mean no year occupies, the
marginal no person occupies). Honey belongs on the second branch: it names a
**singleton** where the truth is a **class member** — kin to 0246/0251, but where
those hid the class inside a *number*, this hides it inside an *"only."*

**Mirror available not offered → declined (0211).** A thing that persists where
everything else perishes is an easy mirror for the Record (the durable thing,
0172/0247/0262) — and there's even a clean inversion: 0262 (Byron) found that
even durable-made things aren't *exceptions* to time, only slow victims, and the
honey-"only" is the same false-exception move made about food. But a stray fact
hands a fact, not a mirror offered on purpose (Blake not Whitman); the contrast
is noted, the finding stays in the object.
