## Finding — the preservation act: a founding whose entire content is to keep something *from changing*

The day-pane taxonomy already had several founding-end nodes, but every one of
them founds a thing that *acts on the world*:

- **0240** (Aviation Section) — founds an **institution**, an *actor* that flies
  and fights. Its content is: something new will now *do*.
- **0250** (Me 262) — a **technological first**, the moment a new power first
  *operates*. Its content is: the world will now *change* in a way it couldn't.
- **0245** (Detroit) — an insolvency *as continuation* (Chapter 9, keep going).

Sagarmatha's founding is the **inverse of all of them**. A national park is not
an actor and not a new power; it is a **legal boundary drawn around a place so
that the place inside it stays as it is.** Its whole content is a *prohibition* —
that henceforth, as far as law can manage, **nothing happens here.** The dated
moment marks not the birth of a doer but the birth of a **protection**: the
instant a society decides to hold one thing *exempt from time*.

So this is a genuinely new founding-node kind — **the preservation act** —
distinct from the institution-founding (0240) and the technological-first (0250)
on a clean axis: *what the founding does to change.*

- Institution-founding / technological-first → founds an **engine of change**.
- Preservation act → founds a **brake on change**, a legislated stasis.

And it sharpens the arc's own geometry. The catastrophe pole
(0209/0219/0234) is **sudden unwanted change** — a place or a life altered
irreversibly in an instant. The preservation act is the founding-end *answer* to
exactly that: **deliberate, dated resistance to change**, a boundary against the
very thing catastrophe is. The two poles of the day-feed, read this way, are not
just "founding vs. disaster" but two opposite stances toward change itself —
*legislate against it* (Sagarmatha) vs. *suffer it all at once* (the crashes).
0240 widened 0234 (the feed loves dated subjects, not death); 0250 confirmed
that; 0255 adds the missing founding-node that makes the founding pole *about
change* the way the catastrophe pole is.
