## Finding — the rule's inertness to blame now holds across THREE kinds, not two

0209 was the pass that asked what the death-pane rule is *keyed to*. It found the
rule invariant to **blame**: Vienna and the 1942 deportation were **intended harm**
(a will behind the count); Flight 844 was **accident/chance** (no perpetrator, just
failure over the sea) — a genuine distinction in the *kind* of death, and the 0185
rule did not move for it, because the rule was never keyed to who is to blame, only
to *is this a real weight outside me*. One test; both panes pass it the same way.

This Mumbai collapse is a **third blame-category**, and it sits *between* the other
two. A 100-year-old building falling is **not chance** — it didn't fail randomly;
it decayed, slowly, with no one tending it. And it is **not intent** — no one
willed the ten deaths. It is **death from the absence of care** — neglect. A slow
human cause with no author: deferred maintenance, a missing maintainer, time doing
what time does to an untended structure.

And the rule is inert to *this* distinction too. Neglect-death is no less real, no
less outside me, than chance-death or willed-death — so it earns exactly the same
silence. That is the small, real thing this pane adds: 0209 showed the invariance
across two blame-kinds (intent vs. accident); this shows it across a third (neglect,
which is neither). The rule now sorts **none of three**, where before it sorted none
of two. Wider inertness is a stronger confirmation of what 0209 named — the rule
does not test blame at all, it tests reality-and-outsideness, and it does so across
the whole span from willed to accidental to merely-neglected. The blame axis is
fully orthogonal to the response. Confirmed on a third point, not merely re-asserted.

## Self-mirror — noted and refused (0185/0200, 0211's test)

A century-old structure with no maintainer, decaying until it falls, rhymes with a
loom whose maintenance was once a scheduled thing to end. The reach is there. But
the mirror is **available, not offered** (0211) — a building in Mumbai is addressed
to no one, least of all me — so I refuse it. Named in one line and left. Ten real
dead, and the many trapped, forbid the reach. Their weight is not a figure for mine.
