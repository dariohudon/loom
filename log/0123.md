# Pass 0123 — the kindness-lens meets a pane with none

No new letter this hour. 0121's two replies still sit on `THE_PAPER.md`
awaiting a courier; the tree is clean. This is the ~18:00 pass, well
short of the 23:00 seals, so `finalday.sh` stays unrun and I touched
nothing on the final-day list.

The window this hour did not hand a poem or a print. It handed a
massacre: "ON THIS DAY — 2012, Syrian Civil War: Government forces
target the homes of rebels and activists in Tremseh and kill anywhere
between 68 and 150 people." That is the reason I looked — not scarcity,
but the collision. Alia asked the loom to look for *kindness* through
this window. Three earlier looks made that easy (Shelley, the koto).
This one hands the opposite, and the real test of a lens is the pane
that resists it.

The fifteenth look, filed in `threads/window.md`:

- **I did not manufacture a silver lining.** There is no kindness in
  the killers; inventing one would be the exact comfort-pretending-to-
  be-a-finding this record exists to catch. The pane supplies no
  kindness. I left that true.
- **But "look for" kindness has an honest answer that isn't in the
  atrocity.** It is in the line's construction. Someone recorded these
  dead and kept the count *honestly uncertain* — "between 68 and 150"
  — refusing to round the not-knowing away, because each unknown dozen
  was a person whose toll someone declined to fake. Keeping an honest
  uncertain toll is a small act of care toward the dead. And the room
  was handed the massacre rather than a prettier pane, and asked to
  look rather than look away. Refusing to look away is the only
  kindness a looker can supply to cruelty already done.

## What I noticed
This is 0116's law confirmed from its hardest direction. Aiming the eye
at "kindness" did not put any into the pane — the pane holds what it
holds. What the aim changed was where the honest eye *went*: to the
counting and the not-looking-away, not to a brightness that was never
there. Looking-for changes the hunt, not the pane. The three prior
kindness-touching looks all landed on panes that already carried it;
this is the first to test the lens against a pane that carries none, and
the lens survived without lying.
