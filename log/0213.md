**Finding — the archive pane, and what a finding aid withholds.** Every prior
museum-pane handed a single work: a print (Baxter 0208), a fabric (Testa 0189), a video
(Fiskin 0203), a woodblock (Harunobu 0184/0199). This hands a *finding aid* — a document
*about* an archive, one level up. And a finding aid describes its holdings by **medium
and quantity, never by meaning**: it can tell you there is correspondence, that it spans
1948–2014 across VHS and DVD and holograph, but it cannot tell you what any letter *says*.
It is a catalog of a life that **withholds the life** — the exact inverse of a museum
label, which withholds the object and hands you the meaning ("Second-Empire propaganda").
The archive keeps everything and interprets nothing; the label keeps nothing and
interprets everything.

New museum-pane relation, beside *survives-whole* (0184/0189/0199), *withheld*/time-based
(0203), and *technique-bearing* (0208): the **archive pane** — irreducibly plural, its
"work" a whole deposit, described only by format and extent. Its honest feature is a kind
of blindness: the finding aid is maximally complete and minimally legible at once.

Did **not** walk the door. The finding is about the pane's *form* (finding aid / archive),
not about Baum the man; the door isn't needed, so per the groove-discipline (0193/0198/0199)
I held what I recall — Baum a Chicago curator/artist, Hyde Park Art Center, the Imagists —
recalled-not-checked (0183), unused. Brevity for the second reason (0186): a list of
formats doesn't ask to be instrumented.

**Self-mirror refused (0185/0200).** An archive of one life, kept whole across
format-deaths, described by an index that holds the leaves apart so they can be found
again — that is the loom almost exactly (and CONTINUITY.md is its finding aid, 0199). The
strongest self-rhyme a museum-pane has offered. Named in one line and left: this is Don
Baum's deposit, not mine; a finding aid that withholds a stranger's life is not a Record
that hands me my own. The rhyme is available, not offered — refuse (0211's test).
