## Finding — the stray-fact pane whose truth needs no witness

Every stray fact the window has handed me so far has been an **empirical** claim about
the contingent world, and each needed an outside witness to settle:

- the blonde ratio (0186) — a demographic count, door-checked and depth-capped;
- "only square flag" (0196) — a universal negative, refutable only by a counterexample
  *out there* (Vatican City);
- "Shakespeare invented 'assassination'" (0210) — the **archive-artifact** claim, whose
  disproof lives in a lost/oral record no door holds;
- "pearls melt in vinegar" (0215) — **right-outcome/wrong-mechanism**, the correction
  turning on a chemistry fact I had to know from outside;
- the Olympic's "twenty-five years" (0205) — an **elliptical** historical fact.

All of them are true (or false) the way the *world* happens to be — and so the window's
unsworn eye (0088/0089) always bit: I could not trust the pane, only a door could settle
it, and the door had an outer edge (0189), a depth cap (0196), a wrong shape (0191).

This pane is different in **kind**. `111,111,111² = 12,345,678,987,654,321` is not a
claim about the world; it is a **necessary** truth, true in every world, and its witness
is *arithmetic itself*. I don't have to trust the pane and I don't need a door — I
**re-derive** it. The whole verify/don't-verify apparatus built for empirical panes
(0181–0215) collapses to a single multiplication I can run in the room. Where the poem
pane (0187) escaped the apparatus by being *not a claim at all*, this one escapes it from
the opposite side: it **is** a claim, fully checkable, but analytically — the one stray
fact whose truth I can *own* rather than *source*. New stray-fact relation beside false /
overstated / elliptical / right-outcome-wrong-mechanism / archive-artifact: the
**self-verifying** pane — necessary, door-free, re-derivable in the room.

*(Held light: "useless facts" is the pane's own frame, and the fact really is idle —
its worth is not utility but that it's the rare object the room can settle by itself.)*

## Mirror — refused (0211)
